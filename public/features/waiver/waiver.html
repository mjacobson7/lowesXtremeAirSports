HAVE FUN, FLY SAFE!

Lowes is designed for everyone, and we want to make sure you fly safe. Lowes Team Members help ensure that rules and regulations are followed. We ask that you become familiar with and abide by the rules below:

  • Remember, stay in your comfort zone!
  • DO NOT ATTEMPT any activity, flip, jump or trick you don’t think you can handle.
  • Flips or other tricks can be dangerous … perform at your own risk.
  • Everyone that enters Lowes Arena must have completed a valid and signed Lowes waiver.
  • Those under 18 must have it signed/completed by their parent, legal guardian, or someone with power of attorney.
